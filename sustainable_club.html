Overview

The Davisburg Sustainable Engineering Club, founded to foster sustainability within the community, led impactful projects such as the Mill Pond renovation.

Mill Pond Renovation Project

This $5 million project involved significant river restoration efforts, with sediment traps set up to pool and filter the river. Over time, we saw the river widen, then narrow, as sediment was removed, allowing for a vibrant and biodiverse environment to develop.

Our team collaborated with Oakland County Parks and Stantec to survey the area, set up sediment traps, and narrow the channel.
